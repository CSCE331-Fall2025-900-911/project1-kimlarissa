Summary

FindAYota is a web app that helps users find Toyota vehicles matching their preferences and budget. Users input their desired features, and the system recommends the top three models while estimating monthly payments. Built with Flask and Tailwind CSS, it offers an intuitive interface with plans to expand features and support more car brands.

Features

  • An interactive UI/UX design
  • Smart calculation of car compatibility
  • Customizable financial planning for each car
  • Intuitive form design
